About

Welcome! For the past year or so, the fans and the media heard and talked a lot regarding Nintendo's next videogame console, code named NX. Now it's officially Nintendo Switch! The first look trailer confirmed a lot of the rumors, but some specs and games are yet to be revealed. Meanwhile, new reports keep coming. So, what we should or not expect to see on january 12th 2017, the date of the next Switch presentation? Take your guesses and generate your bingo cards easily!

Don’t worry if you can’t see the whole sample card in your mobile device. Just generate your card and scroll it horizontally!

Confirmed or disconfirmed options are now disabled. Games shown in the trailer are considered only demos until an official announcement, so their options are still available.

How to play

When a content within a cell of your card is confirmed with an official announcement, mark it as correct. In a real bingo game, the first player to match all cells in the same column, line or diagonal wins. Since this is just for fun, try to match all cells in your card and compare with your friends to confirm who got the greatest number of correct ones!
